Key Lime Pie

Description

A sweet, yet tart key lime pie that can be made in as little as 30 minutes! It can be enjoyed on its own or with your favourite garnishes.

Ingredients

For the pie crust

  • 250g shortbread cookies
  • 85g melted butter

Make the pie crust

  1. Crush the cookies in a ziploc bag as finely as you can
  2. Melt the butter, then mix it into the crushed cookies in a bowl
  3. Shape and compact into a pie dish. No need to bake!

For the pie filling

  • 4 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup key lime juice
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk

Prepare the pie filling

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â° F
  2. Mix all ingredients together into a bowl until well mixed into a semi-thick consistency
  3. Pour onto pie crust
  4. Place pie into oven and bake for 15 mins
  5. Remove from oven and let it cool for at least 30 mins. Then refrigerate for at least 3 hours
