The Last Word

Description

A gin-based cocktail that's easy to make, and easy to modify if you're looking to substitute ingredients while following the ingredient formula.

Ingredients

All equal parts 1 oz:

  • Lime juice (acid)
  • Maraschino Liqueur (sweetener)
  • Green chartreuse (mixer)
  • Beefeater Gin (or gin of your choice; base/hard liquor)

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients into a cocktail shaker
  2. Shake for about 15s
  3. Strain into a coupe glass
  4. Serve, no garnish
