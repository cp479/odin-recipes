Baked Salmon entrée

Description

Simple baked salmon made with lemon and garlic for incredible flavor. Easy to make, whether it's during busy nights or for special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillet (you can prep as a single large fillet, or cut it up into similar sizes)
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Green onion
  • Garlic
  • Parsley (for garnish)
  • Lemon slices/wedges (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375° F
  2. Place the salmon fillet(s) into a baking dish
  3. Add olive oil and lemon juice on top of the fillets
  4. Mince the garlic and add onto the fillet(s)
  5. Chop the green onion into small pieces and sprinkle onto the fillet(s)
  6. Season with salt and black pepper to your liking
  7. Cover the baking dish with foil, and bake in oven for 30 mins
  8. Remove from oven, and remove the foil. Garnish with parsley and lemon slices/wedges
